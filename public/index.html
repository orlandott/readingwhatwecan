The Entry Point (Primers & Essays)

Recent, high-impact essays and accessible books that define the current conversation.

Best for: Beginners looking for a high-level understanding of the stakes without getting bogged down in math or dense academic jargon.

The Canon (Foundational Books)

Major works that established the vocabulary and theoretical groundwork of the field.

Best for: Readers who want a deep, comprehensive dive into the philosophy, strategy, and future scenarios of AGI.

The Problem Space (Research Agendas & Concepts)

Papers defining specific failure modes (misalignment, power-seeking, and gaming) and concrete research targets.

Best for: Researchers and engineers wanting to understand specific definitions of safety, alignment, and risk factors proposed by the field.

The Technical Frontier (Mechanisms & Interpretability)

Cutting-edge engineering work on making models safer, more honest, and more interpretable.

Best for: ML engineers and practitioners looking for applied methodologies (like RLHF or Circuits) and current state-of-the-art research.

Speculative Fiction (AI-Relevant Sci-Fi)

Narratives that explore the texture of living with advanced AI.

Best for: Exploring alignment and long-term AI concepts through narrative and allegory.
